About Me

My name is Catherine. I'm a sophormore, studying Computer Science, at University of Texas at Austin.

Although most of my experience stem from web dev and UI, my passion lies in data science, computer vision, and graphics.

Currently, I am the treasuer and outreach coordinator of Texas Wushu, a student org focused on the art of the Chinese martial arts Wushu. Here, I am charge of the club's budget and funds, as well as spreading awarness to the club and the sport itself.

Besides Wushu, I am also a software developer for the Robotics and Automation Society Region V team. Building a data tracking program and robot's UI are some of the projects I helped create.

Outside of school, I love to create art, specifically graphic design and illustrations, do long-distance running, and study history.
