5.) What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?

My tip-of-the-week is to write to about 20% more than the word requirement for your essay or writing assignments drafts. This tip was actually from my UGS professor. He said that the majority of what you write is superfluous. For instance, writing more allows you to get all the stuff you want to say. Then when you began editing, you realize which sections is important and which section is redundant. I don’t know this is true, I just know that ever since I followed his advice, I receive the highest grades on my essays than I ever did in my entire life. Is it a lot of work and time to follow the advice, but I think it’s worth it.
