CS 371p Spring 2018: Catherine Lu

Week of 2 Apr - 8 Apr Post

Here's my eleventh blog post.

1.) What did you do this past week?

This week was a bit busy. I had a algo exam, religious studies exam, and the OOP project. The algo exam and OOP project was quite rough. Thankfully, the religious studies exam was better, only had a few tricky questions. Yeah, the majority of my week was spent on studying or working on assignments.

Though one silly incident did happen (I guess). On Thursday, I had a scheduled phone interview. When the time came, my phone didn’t ring at all, so I thought the interviewer must have forgot about me. After 30 minutes of waiting, I sent them an email about rescheduling. Thinking back, I should have call them 5-10 minutes after the scheduled time, but I didn’t. Then Friday morning, the interviewer emailed me back. Turns out, she did call me but my phone didn’t pick up her calls. She then asked if I can do an interview today at 10am. Sadly, I woke up at 10:20. So now, we’re trying to schedule an interview for next week. Hopefully, things will go well.

2.) What's in your way?

Nothing major is happening in school next week. A nice break from the hectic two weeks I had before. Still, have to finish my weekly reading assignments for my class.

3.) What will you do next week?

This weekend and next week I plan to practice my interview answers. I haven’t been able to do much after spring break. So I really to some preparation this weekend. Thankfully, there’s isn’t much schoolwork to do, so I can really focus on interview prep. Other than that, I need to finish readings for my religious studies and government class.

4.) What’s my experience of the class?

The material in this class has been interesting and tricky. To keep up, I think I should try studying and reviewing the material everyday.

5.) What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?

My tip-of-the-week is to call you interviewer if it has been maybe 5-10 minutes after the schedule time. Because either they might have forgot or your phone doesn’t like accepting calls at the moment. On Thursday, I didn’t do it as I thought it was rude, especially if the interviewer says they will call you themselves.
